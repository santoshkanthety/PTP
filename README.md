# PTP™ Framework — People • Technology • Process

> **People first. Tech next. Process to scale.**

PTP is a **change-and-adoption operating model** designed for the AI era. It starts with People (align outcomes and roles), makes Technology concrete early (demos and prototypes over promises), and then locks in Process to scale safely.

## 🔗 Live Site

**[santoshkanthety.github.io/PTP](https://santoshkanthety.github.io/PTP)**

## 🧩 The Model

```
People → Technology → Process
  ↑                        |
  └──── Learn & Improve ───┘
```

| Phase | Focus | Key moves |
|---|---|---|
| **People** (Align) | Why & Who | Outcome clarity · Persona mapping · Adoption contract |
| **Technology** (Make it real) | What & How | Workflow mapping · Prototype fast · Trust by design |
| **Process** (Scale) | Run & Improve | Standardize · Govern + guardrails · Measure + iterate |

## 🚀 Why PTP?

- AI adoption fails when people are asked to change around an *abstract promise*
- PTP forces Technology earlier so users can see/feel the future workflow quickly
- Trust and governance are built in from day one — not bolted on at the end
