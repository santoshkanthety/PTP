Context

Why distinctive branding matters

"People Technology Process" is descriptive and difficult to trademark alone. A distinctive coined name paired with PTP creates defensible brand equity.

🛡️
Trademark defensibility
Coined names and suggestive marks receive stronger legal protection than descriptive phrases. A unique name makes registration easier.
🧠
Memorability
Short, evocative names stick. "PTP Loop" or "PTP Catalyst" create instant mental anchors tied to the framework's core value.
📢
Market positioning
A strong brand name differentiates PTP from generic "people process technology" references that saturate the consulting space.
💰
Commercial value
Trademarked frameworks command premium pricing. Think ITIL, SAFe, or Six Sigma — brand identity is inseparable from market value.

Candidates

Six distinctive brand marks

Each name captures a different facet of the PTP model. All pair naturally with the full expansion: People • Technology • Process.

PTP Loop™
The continuous improvement cycle
Emphasizes the feedback loop — Process feeds back into People. Ideal for organizations focused on iterative maturity.
Best for: Ongoing transformation programs
PTP Catalyst™
Accelerating AI adoption
Positions PTP as the spark that accelerates change. Strong action-oriented language that resonates with executive buyers.
Best for: Executive pitch decks & sales
PTP OS™
Your organizational operating system
Frames PTP as a living layer that runs the organization — like an OS for change. Modern, tech-forward positioning.
Best for: Tech-savvy audiences & platforms
PTP Flywheel™
Compounding momentum
Each iteration builds on the last, creating compounding returns. Borrowed from the business flywheel concept (à la Jim Collins).
Best for: Strategy & consulting contexts
PTP Shift™
The paradigm shift in change
Captures the fundamental reordering PTP introduces — technology before process. Bold, transformative messaging.
Best for: Thought leadership & keynotes
PTP Method™
Simple, consultancy-friendly
Clean, professional, and familiar. "Method" signals a structured, repeatable approach — easy to sell into enterprises.
Best for: Enterprise consulting & training

Next Steps

Trademark & brand action plan

Recommended actions to secure and leverage the PTP brand identity.

1️⃣
Select primary mark
Choose one name as the primary brand (recommendation: PTP Catalyst™). Use consistently across all materials.
2️⃣
Trademark search & file
Conduct a comprehensive trademark search (USPTO, IPO, EUIPO). File application in relevant classes (education, consulting, software).
3️⃣
Visual identity
Design a unique logo and consistent stylization. Pair with the PTP color system (indigo, sky, emerald) already established.
4️⃣
Usage guidelines
Create brand guidelines covering: logo usage, typography, ™/® symbol placement, and approved phrasing for all touchpoints.

™ usage indicates intent to claim rights. File with your national trademark office (USPTO, IPO, etc.) for registered ® protection.
